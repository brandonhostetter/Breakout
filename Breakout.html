How to Play

Use the mouse or the arrow keys to control the paddle at the bottom of the page horizontally. Press space to pause the game.

Your goal is to hit and destroy all of the colored bricks at the top. Destroy all of the bricks to progress to the next level.

If the ball passes your paddle, you will lose a life. You are given two lives at the start of the game.
